Synapse Learn

This project, "SynapseLearn," aims to create a dynamic and collaborative online learning platform. It leverages the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js) to build a system where users can engage in real-time document collaboration, participate in categorized study groups and discussions, create and practice with flashcards, and track their learning progress. It's designed for students, educators, and lifelong learners seeking an interactive and supportive learning environment.

NeighbourlyBoost

NeighbourlyBoost" is a platform designed to strengthen local communities by connecting residents with nearby businesses. It allows users to easily discover local shops, restaurants, and services, view detailed business profiles, read reviews, and directly support these businesses through features like virtual tip jars and pre-order systems. This platform aims to foster a thriving local economy by providing businesses with increased visibility and convenient tools to engage with their community
